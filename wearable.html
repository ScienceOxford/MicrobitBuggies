Make your instrument wearable

Once you are happy with your code, you might want to make your micro:bit instrument easier (or more complicated!) to play, by attaching it to your wrist or turning it into a badge.

Think about how you want to play the instrument before you start - this will help you decide what kind of design to create.


Get your materials

If you are in our workshop, ask the session leader to give you some materials to use.

In normal circumstances we would allow you free access to the craft supplies, however with the safety measures in place for COVID-19, please ask if you would like something that has not been provided for you.

You will have:

  • Felt
  • Scissors
  • Needle & sewing thread
  • Glue gun - ask for help if you can't access your nearest plug socket
  • Small speaker with crocodile clips - connect this instead of the large speakers
  • Battery pack - connect this to your micro:bit to make it more portable
  • Safety pins
  • Marker pens
